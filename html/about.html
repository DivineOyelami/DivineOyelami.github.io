We offer incredible opportunities for people from all around the world to work and travel in Canada. You can earn up to $1900 CAD and have a great time in one of the amazing Canadian Camps we work with. It’s what Camp Canada is all about. You just have to be aware of the moose, maple syrup and mounties!

Those who apply with Camp Canada can expect to work in one of many residential camps all across Canada. If you have a sense of adventure then applying with us means you could be working as an outdoor wilderness leader, if not there are other positions including swim instructors, horse riding instructors or dance teachers amongst many others! It really is the perfect way to spend a summer abroad or as part of a gap year abroad. Remember you don’t even need to be a student so we welcome applications from everyone and from all parts of the world.
